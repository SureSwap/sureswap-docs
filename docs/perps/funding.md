# Funding Your Hyperliquid Account

Before you can open a trade, your Hyperliquid account needs a USDC balance. SureSwap handles the deposit flow entirely inside Telegram.

{: .coming_soon }
HL deposits are in early access. If you encounter any issues, please report them in the support channel.

---

## Requirements

- USDC on **Arbitrum** — Hyperliquid's L1 settles on Arbitrum
- If you don't have USDC on Arbitrum yet, see below

---

## Depositing USDC to Hyperliquid

1. From the dashboard tap **Perps Trading** → **Funding**
2. The dashboard shows your current Arbitrum USDC balance and your HL account balance
3. Enter the amount to deposit
4. Review and confirm

Your USDC moves from your SureSwap Arbitrum wallet to your Hyperliquid account.

---

## Don't have USDC on Arbitrum?

From the Funding dashboard, tap **Get USDC on Arbitrum**. This starts a swap flow that automatically routes your chosen token to USDC on Arbitrum, and then deposits it to your HL account in one flow.
